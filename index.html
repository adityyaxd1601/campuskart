A Message from the

Founder

CampusKart · Est. 2025

When I first came across student buying and selling, I noticed something interesting. Every semester, thousands of useful items changed hands — textbooks, calculators, bicycles, electronics, hostel furniture, and countless other essentials. Yet there was no dedicated place for students to connect safely and easily.

Instead, students relied on scattered WhatsApp groups, Instagram stories, and word-of-mouth. Valuable items were often lost in endless chats, buyers struggled to find what they needed, and trust was always a concern.

I believed there had to be a better way.

CampusKart was born from a simple idea: students should have a marketplace built specifically for them.

✅

A place where every user is a verified student.

✅

A place where affordability meets trust.

✅

A place where products find a second life instead of gathering dust or ending up as waste.

Whether you're a first-year student looking for affordable textbooks, someone moving into a hostel who needs furniture for just one semester, or a graduating student hoping to pass on items you no longer need — CampusKart is designed to make those connections simple, safe, and meaningful.

But CampusKart is about more than buying and selling.

It's about building a stronger student community where helping one another becomes effortless, resources are shared responsibly, and every transaction creates value for both people and the planet.

Our Vision

To build the most trusted student marketplace —
one campus at a time.

This is only the beginning. Thank you for being part of this journey.

AP

Aditya Pawar

Founder, CampusKart
